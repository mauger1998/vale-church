How to make a donation / contribution to the church

Standing Orders

You can set up a standing order to the Church's bank account, for regular giving. Please contact Steve de Carteret (Church Treasurer) for a form. Our bank details are NatWest Guernsey, Sort Code 60-09-20, Account Number 67114156.

Online

You can also give by credit / debit card at valechurch.gg/donate Steve would be happy to answer any questions you may have about any methods of giving.

In Person

Located by the steps inside the church, there is a contactless card payment machine to allow donations to be made.
