EVERYONE IS
welcome AT VALE CHURCH

And that includes you

Vale Church is an inclusive church of moderate Catholic tradition in the Church of England

I have been Rector at St Michel du Valle, known simply as the Vale Church, since February 2023, The church has been witnessing to the Christian faith and serving the people of the Vale for over 1000 years. I feel very honoured to be part of this tradition as I get to know the people of the parish of the Vale, the congregation and the many people who attend our Sunday services but also those who attend weddings, baptisms and funerals here or simply visit as they pass by.

Bev Herve (Rector)
