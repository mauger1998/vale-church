History of the church

Vale Church, also known as St Michel du Valle, has been a steadfast presence in the Vale parish for over a millennium. Rooted in the inclusive traditions of the Church of England, we welcome all to join our vibrant community of faith.

Our Journey Through Time

Founded over a thousand years ago, Vale Church has been a witness to the Christian faith and a pillar of service to the people of the Vale. Interestingly, in the past, reaching Vale Church often required a boat journey, highlighting the deep connection our community has maintained with this sacred site. We are proud to have hosted countless Sunday services, weddings, baptisms, funerals, and visitors who pass through our doors.
